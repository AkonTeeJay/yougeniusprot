About YouGenius

With the main aim of raising record breaking students, YouGenius propels its members' zeal for their academics and also promotes relevance for their different skills in the society.
The YouGenius club is an initiative of the Isaiah Wealth ministries birthed from the desire to advance academic excellence, talent and skills of students with drive to succeed and become an influence to the society.

ABJ Reading Programme

This is a structure created by to guide the study life of members. The programme helps to identify the study strenght of each member as well as to encourage and help mebers in areas of weakness

Direct Mentorship

Experienced and trusted advisers committed to guide mentees find success on their chosen path. Mentors are assigned to members depending on compatibility in order to develop strenghts, beliefs and personal attributes.

Academic Seminar

Held once in a semester, the academic seminar is aimed at creating a spark and re-igniting the fire for academic excellence of members. Seminars help focus the mind of the students to achieve their academic goals.

Freshers Orientation

The orientation programme is aimed at the 100-Level students to inform them on the school system, academic projections required for achieving a 1st class, reading plans and general information of the school and its enviroments.

Tutorial Classes

It is the duty of the club to provide tutorial Classes for members found lagging in certain areas of their academics. This is free and open to all interested. Selected mentors and external tutors will be invited for this purpose. To give members the needed boost to excel.
